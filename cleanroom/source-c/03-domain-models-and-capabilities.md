# Source C — domain models and capabilities

## Core IPAM

Hierarchical **subnets**, **addresses**, **VRFs**, **VLANs**, and related filters support IPv4/IPv6 operations typical of enterprise IP planning.

## Physical and logical inventory (lighter than Source A/B)

**Devices**, **locations**, **racks** with printable elevations and maps—enough for many operators without full cable graph modeling.

## Routing and circuits (adjacent)

Screens for **BGP/OSPF** visibility, **circuits**, **NAT**, **multicast**, **PSTN** prefixes—useful where voice/network teams overlap.

## DDI-adjacent

**DHCP** lease/reservation views, **PowerDNS**-related domain and record tooling—**light DDI** assistance, not a full authoritative DNS/DHCP server product.

## Hygiene

**Duplicate** detection, **inactive host** tracking, **firewall zones**, **scanned networks**—day-2 operational visibility.
