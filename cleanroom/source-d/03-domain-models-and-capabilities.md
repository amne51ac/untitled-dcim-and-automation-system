# Source D — domain models and capabilities

## Asset and CMDB core

**assets** and related apps track hardware entities with financial and vendor dimensions (**licences**, **trade_marks**, **supports**).

## Data center and visualization

**data_center** and **dc_view** provide facility-oriented views—racks and placement emphasis comparable in spirit to DCIM UIs in Source A but centered on **asset records**.

## Network services

**dns**, **dhcp**, **domains**, **networks**, **virtual** tie addressing and services to assets—useful alignment but typically **less exhaustive** than Source A’s native IPAM/circuits/cloud object sets for **pure network automation** use cases.

## Operations

**operations** and **notifications** support day-2 processes; **reports** and **dashboards** surface KPIs.

## Security and compliance artifacts

**ssl_certificates**, **security** app—enterprise compliance adjacent concerns.
