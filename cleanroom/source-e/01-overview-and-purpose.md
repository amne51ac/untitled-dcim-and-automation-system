# Source E — overview and purpose

## Mission

Source E delivers **professional IP address management** and, with the **zone management** extension, **DNS zone and record** governance—implemented as **PHP extensions** for **iTop**, an open ITSM/CMDB platform.

## License

## Platform dependency

TeemIP is **not standalone**: it inherits **ticketing, portal, CMDB, organizations**, and workflows from the host **iTop** deployment.
