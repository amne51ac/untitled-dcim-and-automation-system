# Source E — extensibility and iTop platform

## iTop as host

**Modules**, **datamodels**, **menus**, and **profiles** follow iTop extension conventions—operators customize through iTop’s mechanism plus TeemIp-specific classes.

## ITSM features

**Incident**, **change**, and **service** concepts available when the deployment uses full iTop—TeemIp benefits without reimplementing ticketing.

## Multi-organization

Overlapping IP spaces and delegation align with **managed service** and **large enterprise** org trees.

## Contrast with Source A

Source A is a **single monolith** with native network models. Source E is a **layered extension** on a CMDB/ITSM core—integration depth depends on iTop configuration.
