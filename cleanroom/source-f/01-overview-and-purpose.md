# Source F — overview and purpose

## Mission

Source F is a **network monitoring and administration suite**—SNMP-centric discovery, **alerts**, **topology maps**, **metrics**, and **inventory**—aimed at NOC and campus operators. **GPL-3.0** licensed.

## Not a pure SoT

Unlike Source A/B, the product’s **center of gravity** is **operational telemetry** and **fault management**, not only documenting design intent—though it maintains rich inventory derived from polling.
