# Source H — overview and purpose

## Mission

Source H is a **datacenter rack and asset** tool—objects in racks, ports, cables, and attributes—with a **GPL** license and a **long-maintained** PHP codebase.

## Scope

**Narrow DCIM**: strong for **tabular rack** modeling; **not** a network automation hub.

## Audience

Teams needing **structure** without deploying Django-scale platforms—labs, SMB, education.
